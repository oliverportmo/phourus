Stream

All of the content on Phourus can be found on the highly customizable Stream. You can toggle the different types of posts and browse by parameters such as date, location, and Influence to find the kind of information you are interested in.

Whenever you create content on Phourus, you can change privacy settings to customize who sees your post, and who doesn't. This gives you tremendous flexibility with the content you create.

Organizations

Each Element is associated with an Organization type that exists in the REAL world, and not just cyberspace. Orgs also have advanced features not available in the Stream, such as climate & demographic data for Govs, and curriculum data for Schools.

Companies

Phourus Earth

Executives | Employees

Phourus Companies are businesses that wish to connect better with their local community through social media. It is also a platform for these organizations to get exposure they may not otherwise get in mainstream media.

Govs

Phourus Voice

Reps | Citizens

Phourus Govs are local, county and state Governments in the United States. There are approximately 50,000 cities, towns, states and counties in the U.S., and Phourus wants to help them organize better.

Schools

Phourus Mind

Teachers | Students

In addition to the 50,000 local governments in the US, there are over 15,000 school systems. Phourus builds technology to help these Schools organize better while also connecting them with their local community.

Groups

Phourus Self

Leaders | Members

Phourus Groups encompass religious institutions, charities, social service groups and other 'higher purpose' organizations who believe in open expression, and are proud to share their belief system with the Phourus community.


When using Organizations on Phourus, you can see content created from members of that Organization.

Influence

Influence is a numeric rating given to nearly every post, user and organization on Phourus. This score indicates to other users on Phourus how credible something is based on the number of views, comments, thumbs etc.

The idea behind Influence is that the best material rises to the top, making Phourus essentially a competition for creating valuable content.
