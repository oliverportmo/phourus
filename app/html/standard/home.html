What is Phourus

Phourus is both a software solution and a social network. It is broken up into 4 sections called Elements, which are explained in more detail below.

Why Phourus

The U.S. Government is built on a system of 'checks' and 'balances', where each unit of Government keeps other units 'in check'. Phourus is an application 'for us', meaning citizens, to be represented more accurately in local, county and state Government.

The 4 Elements

Society is a delicate balance between economy & environment, education, government & politics, and the belief systems of it's citizens. Phourus is a social community that breaks these components of society into 4 sections called Elements

Stream

All of the content on Phourus can be found on the Stream. The Stream is where you can filter all of the content on Phourus to find the kind of information you are interested in. You can browse by parameters such as date, location, and Influence, toggle the different types of posts, and also select what users show up in your Stream.

Whenever you create content on Phourus, you can change privacy settings to customize who sees your post, and who doesn't. This gives you tremendous flexibility with the content you create.

Organizations

Each Element is associated with an Organization type that exists in the REAL world, and not just cyberspace.

Phourus Earth

Companies

Executives | Employees

Phourus Companies are businesses that wish to connect better with their local community through social media. It is also a platform for these organizations to get exposure they may not otherwise get in mainstream media.

Phourus Voice

Govs

Reps | Citizens

Phourus Govs are local, county and state Governments in the United States. There are approximately 50,000 cities, towns, states and counties in the U.S., and Phourus wants to help them organize better.

Phourus Mind

Schools

Teachers | Students

In addition to the 50,000 local governments in the US, there are over 15,000 school systems. Phourus builds technology to help these Schools organize better while also connecting them with their local community.

Phourus Self

Groups

Leaders | Members

Phourus Groups encompass religious institutions, charities, social service groups and other 'higher purpose' organizations who believe in open expression, and are proud to share their belief system with the Phourus community.

When using Organizations on Phourus, you can see content created from members of that Organization.

For example, if you select a School, you can see all the Subjects, Questions and Answers created by Teachers and Students from that school.

If you select a Gov, such as the city of Boston, Massachusetts, you can see all Debates, Bills and Votes from Reps and Citizens within Boston.

In addition, all Organizations have advanced features that are not available in the Stream.

Influence

Influence is a number rating given to nearly every post, user and organization on Phourus. It is based on an algorithm that takes parameters such as number of views, comments, and likes to come up with a numeric score from 0 to 100. This score indicates to other users on Phourus how credible a post or user is.

The idea behind Influence is that the best material rises to the top, making Phourus a competition for creating valuable content.
